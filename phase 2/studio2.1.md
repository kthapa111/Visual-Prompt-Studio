## Prompt A – Charcoal Drawing

Create a charcoal drawing of an elderly woman sitting by a window. Use strong lines, texture, and contrast to emphasize the wrinkles and expression of her face. Use cross-hatching, smudging, and tonal shading to build depth and shadows. The composition focuses on the woman’s calm expression while light from the window creates emphasis.

## Prompt B – Linocut Print

Create a linocut print of an elderly woman sitting by a window using bold carved lines, strong contrast, and simplified shapes. The design emphasizes texture, line, and contrast to highlight wrinkles and expression. High-contrast black and white areas create emphasis and movement.
