# Visual Prompt Studio 1.1

## Prompt
Create a powerful protest poster that serves as a social protest about affordable housing.
Location/Place: The poster is displayed around a busy downtown city area where many working-class families live.
Social Role: The artwork should raise awareness and encourage viewers to question rising rent prices and housing inequality.
Meaning: The message is that everyone deserves a safe and affordable place to live.
Subject Matter: Include a realistic image of a small family standing in front of a tall apartment building with a large “FOR RENT” sign showing an extremely high price. Show contrast between luxury high-rise buildings and a struggling family holding moving boxes. Include bold protest text that says “Housing is a Human Right.”
Style: Representational and realistic, with strong contrast and emotional facial expressions to emphasize urgency and injustice.

## Vocabulary Breakdown
Medium: Protest poster (digital illustration)
Meaning: The artwork communicates that housing is a human right and criticizes rising rent and housing inequality.
Subject Matter: A struggling family holding moving boxes, a luxury apartment building, an expensive rent sign, and bold protest text.
Style: Representational.
